Who I Am

Thanks for coming to my website! I am an undergraduate at the University of Wisconsin-Madison in Cartography/GIS. My education in the field of mapmaking has given me the opportunity to also become a computer programmer, web developer, and graphic designer.


I have developed both my interactive and static cartography skills in not only course work, but in professional experience as well. With my background in programming through Java, as well as JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and jQuery, I want to create design that is thought provoking, clean, and efficient.
