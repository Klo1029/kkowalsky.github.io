Where in the World is Lichtenstein?

Click on a marker to see where the master of pop art paintings has his work.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

This map was quickly made using my Lichtenstein Tileset and Mapbox Editor for research under Dr. Robert Roth at UW-Madison. I'm walking through teaching students with no javascript experience how to make a slippy map that's highly stylized. The data I compiled is from the Lichtenstein Foundation, of all the locations in the world you can see one of Roy's pieces of art.
